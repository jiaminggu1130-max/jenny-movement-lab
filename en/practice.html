7-Day Body Awareness Practice for Women

Ten minutes a day, returning to the body

Pause, make a movement smaller, or rest whenever you need. There is no standard to keep up with; you decide what completion means.

DAY 1

Where am I?

Begin with breath and the soles of your feet, bringing attention gently into the body.

BreathFeet awarenessBody scanGroundingA sense of safety
The practice video will appear here

After practice, ask yourself

Which part of your body is easiest to notice right now?

DAY 2

What can I notice?

Without rushing to change anything, notice ease and tension in the neck, ribs, and pelvis.

Neck & shouldersRibcagePelvisNoticing tension
The practice video will appear here

After practice, ask yourself

Where is there ease, and where might you need more time?

DAY 3

I can move

Explore simple weight shifts in every direction and discover new possibilities for movement.

Weight shiftsFoundational stepsMoving with less holding
The practice video will appear here

After practice, ask yourself

Which direction feels most steady today?

DAY 4

My pelvis belongs to me

Let pelvic movement belong to the body: it is about support, connection, and choice—not performance.

Pelvic mobilityCore supportSpinal connection

After practice, ask yourself

How would you move your pelvis if there were no one to impress?

DAY 5

I can take up space

Use direction, steps, and reaching arms to let your body take up space.

StepsDirectionArm reach
The practice video will appear here

After practice, ask yourself

DAY 6

I can have rhythm

Explore coordination through a simple Latin pulse, letting rhythm become something you can feel.

Latin rhythmCoordinationWeight & beat

After practice, ask yourself

DAY 7

I choose how to express

Bring the week together in a short sequence, choosing your own size, pace, and way of moving.

Simple sequenceFree movementPersonal choice

After practice, ask yourself

You do not need to be thinner, sexier, or technically perfect to feel at home in your body.
